About Us

We are dedicated to empowering women with health insights using data-driven models. Our prediction tool is based on the Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset and is designed for educational and preventive purposes. Our team includes data scientists and health experts committed to women's wellness.

We are Arnav Tyagi, Krish Aneja, Anmol Jindal, and Abhiuday, students of BCA 5th Semester, Section F. Together, we developed a website focused on early diabetes prediction specifically for women. Our project combines our academic knowledge and technical skills to create a platform aimed at raising awareness and facilitating timely detection of diabetes risk in women. By integrating data analysis and user-friendly web technology, our goal is to empower women with actionable health insights at an early stage. This initiative reflects our commitment to leveraging technology for improving health outcomes and making a meaningful societal impact through innovation and teamwork.
