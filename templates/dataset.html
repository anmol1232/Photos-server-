Dataset Information

The Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset includes 768 instances with 8 medical predictor variables and 1 target variable (Outcome: 0 = No Diabetes, 1 = Diabetes). It was collected from Pima Indian women aged 21 and above. Learn more about it here.
